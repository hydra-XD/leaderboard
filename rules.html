Glitchless vs Glitched
Glitchless runs feature no glitches aiding speed or skipping any portion of the level. Game bugs that do not benefit the player (Up/floater glitch, gravity glitch) are allowed. Glitched runs use game exploits to skip portions of a level or increase speed beyond what is intended by the game. Game bugs that benefit the player (Manual or automatic superbounce, deathclips, menu smuggling, storage manipulation) are allowed.
